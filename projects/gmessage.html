GMessage

A slight preamble to the project: GMessage is project borne out of pure passion and utter frustration. I am not shy about my love for Google, and I have always had a passion about making ubiquitous experiences (like messaging) easier and more fun to use. Google's troubles in this space opened up a perfect opportunity for me. There's the passion. The frustration has come from my junior year of education at Pratt Institute. Wanting to be an interactive designer, I've found Pratt almost refuses to teach me and give me projects that I think are important to becoming well versed in the digital space. Therefore, I created my own project -- not only to teach myself, but to show I know what I can do. Directly below is the annotated deck of designs if you really want to pick my brain.

Anyways, here's wonderwall.
