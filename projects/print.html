Review

Review is a book (and accompanying video piece) about subtext. How does one bring what is unimportant to the forefront of thought? What is important anyway? How do we assign value to things? The book is ultimately a collection of assembled works and original writings touching on these ideas.

The book was assembled and typeset by myself. Physical book can be viewed upon request.

COMD 15

Brief: Study and research Pratt's "COMD 15" typefaces. After our research, we were assigned a typeface to write an essay on and design a zine around. I was given Adobe Garamond, and I wrote my essay on how Garamond's history relates to current typographic trends.
